
## Break the Bricks
Welcome to Break the Bricks! This game offers an exciting gameplay experience where you control a paddle to bounce a ball and break bricks. Your objective is to break all the bricks on the screen while avoiding letting the ball fall below the paddle.

![image alt](https://reynardchristiansen.github.io/porto/assets/project-5.jpg)

## Features

- Modern UI with particles, ball trail, and sound effects
- Responsive & mobile-friendly controls
- 3 levels with increasing difficulty
- Bonus Ball combo for extra balls

## Gameplay

Controls:

- **Desktop:** Arrow keys or A/D to move · Space to launch · P to pause
- **Mobile:** Drag anywhere on the board to move · Tap to launch · on-screen ◄ ► buttons
- **Mouse:** Move the cursor over the board and the paddle follows

Objective:

- Break all the bricks by bouncing the ball off the paddle.
- Prevent the ball from falling below the paddle to avoid losing a life.
- Clear all 3 levels to win.

Bonus Ball:

- Break **3 bricks within 10 seconds** to trigger a **Bonus Ball** drop — 3 extra balls fall in from below the bricks, giving you 4 balls at once.
- More balls means more bricks cleared at once, but you only lose a life when the **last** ball drops.
- After it triggers there is a **20-second cooldown** before it can happen again.

Scoring:

- Your score increases as you break bricks (10 × current level per brick).
- You have three lives. Losing a life occurs when the ball falls below the paddle.
- The game ends when lives reach zero.
